## Your Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To make this notice easy to find, we make it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.

## Collection of Personal Information

When visiting steelafse.com, the IP address used to access the site will be logged along with the dates and times of access. This information is purely used to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement and gather broad demographic information for internal use. Most importantly, any recorded IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

## Email Collection

If you sign up to receive a guide or join our email list, we collect your name and email address. We use this information only to send you the requested guide and occasional emails with seasonal tips, reminders, and promotions. We do not sell, share, or rent your email address to third parties. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.

## Links to Third Party Websites

We have included links on this site for your use and reference. We are not responsible for the privacy policies on these websites. You should be aware that the privacy policies of these sites may differ from our own.

## Changes to This Privacy Statement

The contents of this statement may be altered at any time, at our discretion.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding the website or privacy policy of steelafse.com, you may contact us at Admin@steelafse.com

## Accessibility

We're committed to making our website usable for everyone. We aim for steelafse.com and its main pages to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA.

### What we do

- Use semantic HTML, headings, labels, and alt text
- Maintain sufficient color contrast and visible focus states
- Support full keyboard navigation and logical tab order
- Provide text alternatives for images/media and avoid autoplay
- Test new pages with automated and manual accessibility checks

### If you need help or an alternative format

If you have trouble using our site, or need information in a different format, contact us and we'll help:

- Email: admin@steelafse.com

### Feedback & response time

Tell us about any accessibility barriers. We try to respond within 2 business days.

### Compatibility

This site is designed to work with current versions of major browsers and assistive technologies.

### Third-party content

Some pages may include third-party tools or embedded content that we don't fully control. We'll do our best to provide accessible alternatives when needed.
